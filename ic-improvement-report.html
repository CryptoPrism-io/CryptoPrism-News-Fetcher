Exhibit 3

WS6: Second-Order Residual Features

The BTC residual decomposition (April 9) strips market correlation so downstream models see only idiosyncratic alpha. WS6 extracts structure from the raw residual noise—momentum, mean-reversion, volatility regimes, and conviction weighting.

Feature definitions

res_momentum_3d 72h rolling mean of hourly residuals. Captures short-term alpha persistence.
res_momentum_7d 168h rolling mean. Medium-term trending alpha vs BTC.
res_momentum_14d 336h rolling mean. Longer-horizon outperformance trend.
res_zscore_30d z-score of 24h mean residual vs 30d distribution. Mean-reversion signal.
res_vol_regime 7d residual std / 30d std. Ratio >1 flags expanding volatility (breakout).
res_autocorr_7d Lag-1 autocorrelation over 168h. Positive = trending, negative = mean-reverting.
res_autocorr_14d Lag-1 autocorrelation over 336h. Longer-horizon persistence check.
res_volume_interaction mean(residual * volume_zscore) over 24h. High-conviction alpha.

WS3: Cross-Coin Features

Traditional features look at each coin in isolation. Cross-coin features answer: "how is this coin doing relative to all other coins right now?" This captures relative momentum, market health, and volume dynamics that single-coin time series miss entirely.

Feature definitions

cc_ret_rank_1d Percentile rank of 1d return across all coins. 0 = worst, 1 = best.
cc_ret_rank_7d Percentile rank of 7d cumulative return. Medium-term relative momentum.
cc_vol_rank_1d Percentile rank of volume vs 20d average. Identifies unusual activity.
cc_mktcap_momentum 7d change in market cap rank. Rising = gaining relative value.
cc_breadth_20d Fraction of coins above 20d SMA. Market-wide health indicator.
cc_advance_decline log(advancers / decliners). Directional market conviction.
cc_dispersion Cross-sectional std of 1d returns. High = good for stock-picking.
cc_hhi_volume Herfindahl index of daily volume. Measures rotation vs broad participation.

Why Cross-Coin Features Stabilized the Model

A coin returning +5% means nothing in isolation—if all coins are +10%, it is actually lagging. Rank features normalize signals by market context. The model learns that a high residual momentum during a broad rally (high breadth) is less informative than the same signal during high dispersion. This contextual awareness is what collapsed the val/test IC gap from 0.23 down to 0.02.
